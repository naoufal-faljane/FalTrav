Travel Guides

Practical tips for your journeys

Essential Packing Tips

Roll, Don't Fold

Roll your clothes instead of folding them to save space and reduce wrinkles.

Pack Multi-Purpose Items

Bring items that serve multiple purposes, like a sarong that can be used as a beach towel, scarf, or blanket.

Use Packing Cubes

Organize your suitcase with packing cubes to maximize space and easily find items.

What to Pack

  • Travel documents (passport, visas, tickets)
  • Medications and first aid kit
  • Universal power adapter
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Laundry bag for dirty clothes

7-Day Europe Itinerary

Day 1-2: London, UK

  • Visit the Tower of London and Tower Bridge
  • Explore the British Museum
  • Take a ride on the London Eye
  • Experience the West End theatre district

Day 3: Paris, France

  • See the Eiffel Tower and Arc de Triomphe
  • Stroll along the Seine River
  • Visit the Louvre Museum
  • Enjoy French cuisine in Montmartre

Day 4-5: Rome, Italy

  • Tour the Colosseum and Roman Forum
  • Visit Vatican City and St. Peter's Basilica
  • Toss a coin in the Trevi Fountain
  • Indulge in authentic Italian gelato

Day 6-7: Barcelona, Spain

  • Admire Gaudí's architecture (Sagrada Família, Park Güell)
  • Stroll down Las Ramblas
  • Relax at Barceloneta Beach
  • Experience Spanish tapas culture

Budget Travel Tips

Travel Off-Season

Save money by traveling during shoulder or off-season when flights and accommodations are cheaper.

Use Public Transportation

Save on transportation costs by using buses, trains, and metros instead of taxis.

Eat Like a Local

Avoid touristy restaurants and eat where locals eat for authentic cuisine at lower prices.

Money-Saving Strategies

  • Book flights 6-8 weeks in advance
  • Use travel reward credit cards
  • Stay in hostels or Airbnb accommodations
  • Take advantage of free walking tours
  • Look for city tourism cards with included attractions
