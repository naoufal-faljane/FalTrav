Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Respect and Privacy at FalTrav

At FalTrav, we respect your privacy and strive to provide an enjoyable travel experience without the need to collect personal data.

We Don't Collect Personal Data

We do not collect any personal information about you when visiting our site. You can use the services we provide without the need to submit any personal details.

We Don't Use Cookies for Tracking

We do not use cookies to track your browsing or monitor your online behavior. We only use cookies when absolutely necessary to provide essential site functions.

Email Use

If you contact us via email, we use your email address only to reply to your inquiries. We do not share your email with third parties, nor do we keep it for extended periods after answering your inquiry.

No Ads or Analytics

We do not use any external advertisements or analytics services that track your activity online. We do not share your information with advertising networks or analytics companies.

Content for Informational Purposes Only

All content we provide on the site is intended for informational purposes and to help travelers discover new destinations. We do not use the content for any undisclosed commercial or promotional purposes.

Privacy Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy from time to time. Any material changes will be posted on this page.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us at Faltrav25@gmail.com
